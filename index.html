About Us 🦈

HandinHand Creation is a club of Radwa International School, which is involved in business to enhance the creativity of children and to turn the young minds into entrepreneurship. Our students design and handcraft every piece with love and dedication. From crochet accessories to custom mobile covers, each product represents the entrepreneurial spirit we nurture. By supporting us, you're encouraging the next generation of business leaders!
